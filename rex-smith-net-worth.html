Journey Through Music and Acting

His career journey is a captivating tale of talent, versatility, and persistence.

Born on September 19, 1955, in Jacksonville, Florida, he quickly rose to fame, not just as an actor but also as a singer, leaving an indelible mark on both the Broadway stage and the pop music scene.

Starting in the early 1970s as the lead singer for bands like Tricks and Phaedra, Smith transitioned to the hard rock band Rex, showcasing his musical versatility.

His foray into music peaked with the 1979 hit single You Take My Breath Away, catapulting him into the limelight and cementing his status as a teen idol.
