The 7 Lives of Lea Actress Raika Hazanavicius Wiki | Bio

Raïka Hazanavicius (born in 1994) is a 27-years-old French model and artist who is the daughter of a famous actor, Serge Hazanavicius. Raika got catapulted into fame after landing the lead role of Léa in the Netflix crime drama “The 7 Lives of Lea,” which aired on April 22.

The story involves a girl named Lea who, whenever she sleeps, ends up in the body of some other person. Not only that, but she also travels through time with a motive to save a guy named Ismael.

Hazanavicius is represented by a Paris-based agency called Noma Talents. She has worked as a model for many brands, including Nike’s famous shoe brand. The lady is basically from Paris but currently lives in Trinité, Martinique.

Career

When Hazanavicius was a baby girl, she used to observe her parents and their work life. It impacted her mind positively, and she too decided to become an actress without taking any special favor from her parents. She does not want to use her parents’ reputation to build her career and wants to hustle.

To receive the proper training required to become an actor, she joined David Barouk’s Method Acting Center. Here, she learned different acting techniques like Improv, Stella Adler, Practical Aesthetics technique, etc.

In the beginning, Raika’s talent manager, Laurence Joyard, got her a few modeling jobs that she accepted instantly. Hazanavicius also gave multiple auditions and got a few roles in productions like “One Last Summer” and “Very Ordinary People” but could not find herself a leading role.

Then in 2021, came Raïka’s first chance to perform the lead role when she got the offer to work in Charlotte Sanson’s web series “As 7 Vidas De Lea,” which we all know as “The 7 Lives of Lea.”

She also worked in her uncle Michel Hazanavicius’ comedy zombie movie “Final Cut,” which is expected to release on June 15, 2022.
