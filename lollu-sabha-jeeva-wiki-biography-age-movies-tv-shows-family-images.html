Jeeva is well known for his performance in the popular Vijay tv show Lollu Sabha. He started his journey in 2003 along with the famous actor Santhanam. His comedy skills and acting were recognized by many directors. He made his debut film in Vegam along with the actor Ashwin Shekhar. Jeeva is currently playing a main role in Lollu Sabha on Netflix.

Jeeva did some main character and supporting roles in Tamil films. Further, he appeared as a guest in various comedy reality shows like Athu Idhu Yedhu, Kalakka Povadhu Yaaru, and more in Star Vijay. He played a supporting role in Nakkhul and Shanthanu Bhagya Raj’s film Amali Thumali. Jeeva is a fan of superstar Rajinikanth.
