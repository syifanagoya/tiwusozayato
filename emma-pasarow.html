Emma Pasarow Facts

  • In May 2012, she made her first TV show appearance as Tween Girl in an episode titled Zone of Exclusion of the thriller drama television series Touch.
  • Emma Pasarow brought to life the character of Courtney in the 2022 comedy-drama film Am I OK?, starring Dakota Johnson, Sonoya Mizuno, Jermaine Fowler, Kiersey Clemons, Molly Gordon, and Sean Hayes.
  • In 2022, she was cast in the recurring role of Penny in the anthology drama television series Super Pumped.
  • Alongside Belmont Cameli, Kate Bosworth, Dermot Mulroney, and Andie MacDowell, Emma Pasarow co-starred as Auden in the 2022 Netflix drama film Along for the Ride. It is based on the novel of the same name by Sarah Dessen.
